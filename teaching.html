Virtual Econ Simulations

With Matthew Lang, I co-developed Virtual Econ — a website providing Python-based interactive simulations for principles of microeconomics and macroeconomics students. We are actively adding new games and welcome comments and suggestions.

If you are interested in a leaderboard for your class, or would like examples of assignments that pair with each simulation, please contact me. The service is currently free, with the intention of increasing traffic; we hope to eventually fund server costs through private sponsors.
