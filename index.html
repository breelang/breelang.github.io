About

I am a Professor of Teaching in the Economics Department at the University of California, Santa Barbara. My research interests include urban economics, subsidized housing, public finance, and taxation.

In addition to my research, I develop interactive simulations and games for economics courses. With Matthew Lang, I co-created Virtual Econ, a website providing Python-based simulations for principles of microeconomics and macroeconomics students.

In my spare time, I enjoy bird photography. You can find my work on Instagram.
